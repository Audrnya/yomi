Yes... Unfortunately, the image scanner is not perfect and is prone to mistakes. It is also configured to read horizontal Japanese text only. Please keep these details in mind.

Alternatively, Google Images is incredibly accurate and can even read vertical right-to-left Japanese. I recommend having it in another tab in case Yomi's scanner makes errors.
